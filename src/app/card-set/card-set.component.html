Rainbow Writing

Rainbow writing is a team game for a brainstorming session. In this group game,efforts are made to find a conclusion for a specific problem by gathering a list of ideas spontaneously contributed by its members. Complete your rainbow writing and save your link to see the cards and access it anytime .

How it works ?

  • Create a game name to join with other players.
  • Wait for the other players to join to start the game.
  • You will have 7 brainstorming card :
    Functionality
    Application
    Technology industry
    Weakness
    Customer
    Needs
    Trends & Issues
  • Each player need to fill out the card by playing order.
  • When the game is completed, save your rainbow writing cards Link ^^
